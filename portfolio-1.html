"Developed a fully responsive and modern website for [charms by bee], integrating dynamic UI elements, optimized user experience, and seamless third-party integrations."

Charms by Bee

I had the opportunity to design and develop the Shopify store for Charms by Bee, a handcrafted jewelry brand specializing in elegant and minimal pieces. The client wanted a clean, modern, and feminine look that matched the aesthetic of their products. I focused on building a visually appealing layout with soft tones, smooth transitions, and clear product organization that enhanced the user shopping experience from homepage to checkout.

The website features custom product pages that highlight the details of each charm with high-resolution images, variant selectors, and trust-building elements like customer reviews and secure checkout badges. I integrated essential eCommerce features including responsive mobile design, optimized speed performance, and apps for order tracking, product reviews, and email marketing. Special attention was given to SEO structure and easy backend management for the client to update content and add new products effortlessly.

Working on Charms by Bee helped me refine my skills in Shopify Liquid coding, theme customization, and user-centric design.

The store reflects the brand’s identity while maintaining eCommerce best practices to drive conversions. The client was highly satisfied with the final result, and the store is now fully functional, visually aligned with the brand, and ready to scale.
